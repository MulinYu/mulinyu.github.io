Mulin Yu 于沐霖

 

I'm a Postdoc at Shanghai AILab in the IDC research group. I received my PhD from INRIA in 2022, supervised by Prof. Florent Lafarge. the analysis and modeling of 3D environments from physical measurements, typically multi-view imagery and laser scanning. My favourite topics include surface reconstruction, city modeling, novel view synthesis and spatial point processes.
